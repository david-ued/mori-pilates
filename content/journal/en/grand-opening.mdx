Mori Pilates is officially open.

On the eighth floor on Jinshan South Road, we have prepared a quiet space: a full suite of Pilates apparatus, a Japanese Seitai treatment room, and an hour that belongs entirely to you.

## What we offer

- **Private 1:1 sessions** — fully tailored to your body
- **Duet 1:2 / Trio 1:3 small groups** — progress steadily and safely, together
- **Japanese Seitai** — gentle hands-on realignment, alone or paired with Pilates

## How to book

All sessions are by appointment via our [official LINE account](https://line.me/R/ti/p/@326euwbd):

1. Add Mori Pilates on LINE
2. Tell us about your body and what you hope to improve
3. We will match you with the right teacher and time

> Bookings for the following month open on the 20th; please cancel by 21:00 the day before.

We look forward to seeing you in the forest.
